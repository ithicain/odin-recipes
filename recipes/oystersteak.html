Oyster Steaks

Description

If you are interested in foraging your own food and live in a region where Oyster Mushrooms are plentiful, consider grilling your finds for a delicious meaty entree.

Ingredients/Tools

  • Oyster mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 gloves garlic, chopped

Steps

  1. Harvest and clean your mushrooms. Try your best to brush off dirt, rather than rinsing so the mushrooms do not soak up too much water.
  2. Dry sautee mushrooms in cast-iron pan to allow extra water to escape. Drain liquid.
  3. Once no more water is escaping, add 2 tbsp. butter to pan with chopped garlic.
  4. Add salt/pepper.
  5. Sautee mushrooms on med/high heat until browned.
