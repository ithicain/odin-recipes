Turkey Tail Tincture

Description

If you are interested in medicinal foraging and live in a region where Turkey Tail Mushrooms are plentiful, consider adding your finds into favorite soups to enjoy this beneficial tincture.

Ingredients/Tools

  • 5 Turkey Tail mushrooms
  • Soup of choice

Steps

  1. Harvest and clean your mushrooms. Try your best to brush off dirt, rather than rinsing so the mushrooms do not soak up too much water.
  2. Allow turkey tail mushrooms to dry for 5 days in dish.
  3. Put together your soup as desired; at boiling stage, add turkey tail into soup and allow steeping to begin.
